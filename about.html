About Me

I watch so you don't have to.

Who am I? I'm the grumpy old man, of course! I've been in everything from huge Hollywood blockbusters to Aussie dramas to treading the boards at some of the largest and smallest theatres.

I've been in the industry for over 50 years, and have seen & done it all. I've worked as an actor, a director, a consultant, and a writer, but that horrible Covid really forced me to slow down and put my feet up. It was then that I realised I had a great passion for discussing movies and TV shows with others, and was unable to, being locked in my house with only my poodle to look after me.

So I made this website. With the help of my trusty sidekick (editor and web developer), I've built this website to start the discussion!
